## Content Reuse & Efficiency Strategy

The single marketer + AI model works if content is systematically repurposed:

1. **Benchmark Report** (1 asset) becomes: landing page, 5+ LinkedIn posts, 2 blog posts, 3 newsletter references, carousel, conference talking points. This is the content engine for the entire 8 weeks.

2. **CEO LinkedIn posts** are drafted by the content marketer and approved by CEO. Target: 15 minutes CEO time per post (review + personalize), 20 minutes on engagement days.

3. **Blog-to-social pipeline:** Every blog post generates 2--3 social posts (pull-quote graphic, stat highlight, question reframe). AI assists with variant generation.

4. **Newsletter recycles:** Each newsletter edition curates 2--3 existing assets with fresh framing. Minimal net-new writing required.

5. **AI-assisted production:** Use AI for first drafts of blog posts, social copy variants, carousel text, email subject line testing, and repurposing long-form into short-form.

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| CEO availability drops during conference/launch | Pre-write all CEO posts 5+ days ahead. She only needs to review, personalize, and hit publish. |
| Report not ready by Week 2 | Shift report launch to Week 3 (pre-conference). Extend problem-awareness content through Week 2. Conference + report = combined authority moment. |
| Low engagement on launch day | CEO personally DMs 20--30 target personas the morning of launch with a personal note + link. Organic seeding > hoping the algorithm works. |
| Content marketer burnout at Week 5 | Batch-produce Week 5--6 content during Week 3--4. Launch week should be execution (publish, engage, monitor), not creation. |
| No customer testimonials available at launch | Use beta tester quotes, design partner feedback, or the benchmark report data as proxy social proof until real customer stories emerge in Weeks 7--8. |
