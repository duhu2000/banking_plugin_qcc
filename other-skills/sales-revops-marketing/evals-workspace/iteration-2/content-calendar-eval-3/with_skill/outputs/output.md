## PILLAR-CLUSTER ARCHITECTURE

The whitepaper has three sub-themes. Each generates a cluster of derivative content:

**Cluster A -- Predictive Analytics Adoption:** How supply chain teams are (and aren't) using predictive analytics to anticipate disruptions. Angles: adoption gaps, maturity benchmarks, practical frameworks.

**Cluster B -- Real-Time Tracking Maturity:** The gap between what leaders say they need and what their current systems deliver. Angles: the 73% stat, Tier 2+ blind spots, cost of delayed visibility.

**Cluster C -- Supplier Risk Scoring:** How leading organizations score and monitor supplier risk vs. how most still rely on annual audits. Angles: risk scoring methods, automation potential, regulatory drivers.

---

## DOWNLOAD-TO-PIPELINE FLOW

All content CTAs drive to the whitepaper download gate. Post-download flow:
1. Download triggers immediate confirmation email with whitepaper link
2. Contact enters ABM nurture sequence (5-touch, 28-day)
3. Lead scoring runs on download + firmographic match + engagement signals
4. HOT leads (score 80+) route to sales within 24 hours
5. WARM leads (score 60-79) continue nurture with webinar invitation at Touch 3
6. Webinar attendees receive post-webinar follow-up with demo CTA and whitepaper as reference asset
