## Production & Sequencing Logic

**Front-loading principle:** Highest-leverage pieces ship first:
- Week 1: Launch blog + email blast to 8K list + all social channels activated (maximum initial reach)
- Weeks 2-3: Deep-dive blogs on highest-value themes + webinar promotion + second email
- Week 4: Webinar as mid-funnel conversion event (converts engaged audience to deeper engagement)
- Weeks 5-6: Post-webinar momentum + lower-leverage channel fill (FAQ, recap, evergreen content)

**Production deadlines:** All content has a production deadline set at least 5 business days before publish date (visible in the calendar above). Week 1 assets are complete by the prior Wednesday at latest, giving the full prior week as buffer for revisions and approvals.
