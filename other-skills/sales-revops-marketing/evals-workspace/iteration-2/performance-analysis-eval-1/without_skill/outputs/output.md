## 5. Tactical Fixes (Non-Budget)

These cost nothing but could unlock 3-5 additional SQLs in the remaining 4 weeks:

### 5a. Webinar Follow-Up Sprint (This Week — Urgent)
- The 124 engaged attendees minus the 14 who requested demos = **110 warm contacts** who sat through 30+ minutes of your content and did NOT take action.
- Deploy a 3-touch sequence: personal email from the presenter (Day 1), LinkedIn connect + message (Day 2), SDR phone call (Day 3-4).
- Expected yield: 5%–10% conversion to meeting = 5–11 additional meetings, likely 2–4 SQLs.
- **This is the single highest-ROI action available right now.**

### 5b. Email SDR Response Time Audit
- Cold outbound produced 22 replies but only 6 meetings (27% conversion).
- Audit: How fast are SDRs responding to replies? If reply-to-first-response is >2 hours, that is likely the bottleneck. B2B email reply response should be <30 minutes during business hours.
- Fix the process, not the email.

### 5c. MQL Nurture Sequence Overhaul
- The warm nurture is underperforming. With 4 weeks left, you have time for one revision.
- Test: Replace the next email in the sequence with a direct, specific offer — a personalized 15-minute diagnostic call rather than generic content. Warm lists respond to specificity and low-commitment CTAs.
- Consider adding a phone touch to MQLs who have opened 2+ emails but not replied.

### 5d. LinkedIn Creative Refresh
- With reduced LinkedIn budget, the remaining spend should go to retargeting only (website visitors, webinar registrants, email engagers).
- Swap the creative to social proof format: customer quote + metric. The current creative is likely a generic value prop that is getting ignored in feed.
