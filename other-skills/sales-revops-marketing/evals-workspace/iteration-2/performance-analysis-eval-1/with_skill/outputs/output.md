### 4. COLD OUTBOUND EMAIL -- IMPROVE REPLY-TO-MEETING CONVERSION

**Analysis:** Email Campaign 1 has solid fundamentals: 34% open rate (high end of average), 6.17% click rate (good). But the reply rate of 0.92% and meeting rate of 0.25% are merely average for cold outbound. 22 replies from 2,400 sends yielded only 6 meetings -- a reply-to-meeting conversion of 27.3%.

**Diagnosis:** Two issues: (a) 27.3% reply-to-meeting conversion means 16 of 22 replies did not convert to meetings. Some are "not interested" replies, but the ratio suggests the follow-up to positive replies is not fast or compelling enough. (b) The gap between 6.17% click rate and 0.92% reply rate indicates that 125 people clicked a link but did not reply. These are high-intent leads being lost at the CTA step -- the email likely asks for too much (a meeting) when the click behaviour suggests they want more information first.

**Change:**
- **Fix the CTA mismatch:** For clicked-but-no-reply leads, trigger a follow-up email within 24 hours that acknowledges the content they engaged with and offers a lower-commitment next step: "I saw you checked out [resource]. Here's a 2-minute case study showing how [similar company] used this. Worth a quick look?" with the meeting ask moved to email 3 of the sequence, not email 1.
- **Fix reply handling:** Audit the 16 non-converting replies. Categorise them: (a) "not interested" -- respect and remove, (b) "not now / timing" -- add to nurture with a 30-day re-engage, (c) "tell me more" -- these should convert to meetings at 80%+. If "tell me more" replies are dropping, the SDR response time or quality is the bottleneck.
- **Volume:** If ICP list allows, increase sends from 2,400 to 3,000-3,500/week in Weeks 5-8 to compensate for the SQL deficit.

**Owner:** SDR team lead (reply handling), marketing ops (click-trigger automation)
**Deadline:** Click-trigger follow-up sequence built by end of Week 5. Reply audit completed by Friday this week.
**Expected:** Reply rate improvement from 0.92% to 1.3-1.5%. Meeting-to-reply conversion from 27% to 40%+. Net impact: 3-5 additional meetings per week, contributing 1-2 incremental SQLs.
