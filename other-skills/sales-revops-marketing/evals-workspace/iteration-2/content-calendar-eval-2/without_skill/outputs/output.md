# Q1 Quarterly Content Calendar -- Cybersecurity Consultancy for Financial Services

## Context & Constraints Acknowledgement

**Company:** Cybersecurity consultancy targeting CISOs and IT Directors at financial services firms (banks, insurance, fintech)
**Goal:** Thought leadership driving inbound discovery calls
**Resource:** 1 part-time content person (also handles event logistics) -- realistic capacity: 1-2 pieces/week
**Approved channels:** LinkedIn company page + monthly blog post ONLY
**Dropped channel:** Email newsletter -- dropped due to prior year inability to sustain cadence. We will not plan for email to avoid resource waste on a channel the team has already proven they cannot maintain.
**Not planned:** Webinars, paid social, CEO LinkedIn personal brand, podcasts

---

## Workload Calculation

- **Quarter duration:** 13 weeks
- **Max capacity:** 2 pieces/week = 26 pieces theoretical max
- **Realistic capacity (accounting for event logistics):** 1-2 pieces/week with 3 designated buffer/lighter weeks = **19-22 total pieces**
- **Pillar assets:** 3 long-form pieces (spaced ~4-5 weeks apart), each requiring heavier production weeks
- **Derived pieces per pillar:** 2-4 (reduced from ideal 4-8 cluster to match capacity)
- **Tradeoff acknowledgement:** A full pillar-cluster model would produce 4-8 derivatives per pillar. With our part-time constraint, we cap at 2-4 derivatives per pillar, accepting reduced SEO cluster density in exchange for sustainable, consistent publishing.

---

## Content Repurposing Strategy

Each **pillar asset** (long-form blog post, 1,500-2,000 words) gets broken down as follows:

1. **Blog post** (the pillar itself) -- published on company blog
2. **LinkedIn post #1** -- Key insight or stat from the pillar, written as a standalone thought leadership post (200-300 words)
3. **LinkedIn post #2** -- A "hot take" or contrarian angle from the pillar content
4. **LinkedIn post #3** (if capacity allows) -- A mini-case-study or scenario drawn from the pillar topic

This means the writer produces **one major asset** and gets **3-4 channel outputs** from it.

**Production rule:** All content drafts are due **5 business days before publish date** to allow for review, edits, and scheduling.

## Persona & CTA Audit

Every piece in this calendar has:
- **Persona assignment:** Either CISO or IT Director (or both) at financial services firms (banks, insurance, fintech)
- **Conversion-oriented CTA:** Each piece drives toward a discovery call, security assessment, compliance consultation, or vendor risk review -- all inbound pipeline actions
- **Industry specificity:** Topics address cybersecurity concerns specific to financial services (regulatory compliance, vendor risk in banking, supply chain threats to financial institutions, SEC disclosure rules) -- not generic cybersecurity thought leadership

---

## Production Timeline Rules

1. **Draft due:** 5 business days before publish date (minimum)
2. **Review cycle:** 2 business days for stakeholder review
3. **Final edits + scheduling:** 1 business day
4. **Pillar asset weeks:** Writer focuses exclusively on the pillar -- no other content production that week
5. **Buffer weeks:** Lighter derivative content only -- writer has bandwidth for event logistics
