## TESTING RECOMMENDATION

**Lead A/B Pair:** Primary Hero (Outcome-Led: "23% more pipeline when coaching changes behaviour") vs. Alternative A (Pain-Led: "Conversation intelligence shows you what went wrong. It never fixes it.")

**Expected Winner:** Alternative A (Pain-Led)

**Reasoning:** VP Sales and Heads of Sales Enablement at companies with 20--100 AEs have almost certainly already invested in conversation intelligence tooling (Gong, Chorus, or similar). The pain of having spent budget on call recording without seeing rep improvement is visceral and specific to this persona -- they own quota attainment and they've sat through quarterly business reviews where their "intelligence" investment shows no lift in close rates. The pain-led H1 names the exact gap they experience daily ("shows you what went wrong, never fixes it"), which creates immediate recognition and positions Clarion as the solution to a failed category they already bought into. The outcome-led variant is strong but leads with a stat the visitor hasn't yet earned trust in; the pain frame earns that trust first by proving Clarion understands the problem before claiming the result.

**Primary Metric:** Conversion rate (hero CTA click-through to next step -- demo booking or trial signup)

**Secondary Metric:** Bounce rate (measures whether the hero copy earns enough relevance to keep the visitor on page)

**Test Duration:** Minimum 2 weeks or 1,000 unique visitors per variant, whichever comes later, to reach statistical significance on conversion rate.
