# GTM Campaign Brief: AI-Powered Pipeline Forecasting Module Launch

## 1. Campaign Overview

**Product:** AI-Powered Pipeline Forecasting Module (new SKU on existing platform)
**Launch Date:** Week 1 (T-0)
**Campaign Duration:** 12 weeks (announcement through end of launch push)
**Total Budget:** $200,000
**Primary KPI:** 60 qualified demos booked in the launch quarter
- Net-new logos: minimum 20 demos
- Cross-sell (install base): minimum 40 demos

**Secondary KPIs:**
- Demo-to-opportunity conversion rate (target: 50%+)
- Cross-sell attach rate among install base (target: 12% of 340 accounts = ~41 accounts engaged)

- **Pain signal:** Already using our CRM analytics -- forecasting is the natural next step; usage data shows accounts with high CRM adoption are prime for forecasting upsell
- **Channels:** Email to install base, in-app notifications, CSM-driven outreach, webinar invitations, blog/podcast
- **Conversion path:** Email/in-app > product landing page > demo request or "add to plan" > routed to farming AEs (4 AEs)

## 4. Sales-Marketing Alignment & Lead Routing

### Lead Scoring Model

| Signal | Points | Segment |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Demo request form submission | 20 | Both -- auto-HOT |
| Webinar attendee + engaged (stayed >30 min) | 15 | Both |
| Whitepaper/asset download | 10 | Both |
| Email click-through (2+ clicks) | 8 | Both |
| ICP company match (500-5,000 emp, B2B SaaS) | 15 | Net-new |
| VP/CRO title match | 10 | Net-new |
| Existing customer on CRM analytics plan | 15 | Cross-sell |
| High CRM usage score (top quartile) | 10 | Cross-sell |

**Scoring tiers:**
- HOT (70+): Immediate demo scheduling -- 24-hour SLA
- WARM (40-69): Nurture sequence, 48-hour follow-up
- WATCH (<40): Continue nurture, re-score on next engagement

### Lead Type Routing

- **Net-new leads (ICP match, no existing account):** Route to hunting AEs (4 AEs, round-robin). Salesloft sequence: "Pipeline Forecasting -- Net New Demo" (3 touches over 5 days: email, LinkedIn connection, follow-up email).
- **Cross-sell leads (existing customer account):** Route to farming AEs (4 AEs, account-matched). Salesloft sequence: "Pipeline Forecasting -- Cross-Sell Upgrade" (2 touches over 3 days: personalized email referencing current CRM analytics usage, follow-up with CSM warm intro).

### Demo Scheduling SLA

- **HOT leads:** Demo scheduled within 24 hours of form submission. AE sends calendar link within 4 hours.
- **WARM leads:** Demo scheduled within 48 hours of qualification. Nurture continues in parallel.
- **Salesloft automation:** Auto-enroll qualified leads into the appropriate sequence. Sequence exits on reply or demo booked.

## 7. Risk & Contingency Plan

### Risk 1: Post-Launch Demand Dropoff (PRIMARY RISK)

**Decision gate:** If demo pace is below 15 demos booked by end of Week 4 (on track for 60 requires ~5/week average, so 20 by Week 4 is ideal; 15 is the floor).

**Metric to evaluate:** Weekly demo booking rate + source attribution (net-new vs. cross-sell vs. partner).

**Corrective actions (triggered at Week 4 if below 15 demos):**
1. **Budget reallocation:** Shift $15,000 from underperforming paid channels to highest-converting channel based on Week 1-4 data. If LinkedIn outperforms Google, consolidate.
2. **Retargeting activation:** Accelerate retargeting spend from $15,000 planned to $22,000 (pull from underperforming channel). Target all landing page visitors + webinar registrants who did not book demo.
3. **Partner acceleration:** Request co-email blast from both Salesforce and HubSpot partners in Weeks 5-6 (originally planned as single sends). Offer reciprocal promotion.
4. **CSM-driven cross-sell push:** Arm all CSMs with demo booking links and 1-click Slack workflow. Offer existing customers early-access pricing if they demo by Week 8.

### Risk 2: Net-New Demos Below 20

**Decision gate:** If net-new demos are below 8 by end of Week 6 (on track requires ~10 by Week 6).

**Corrective actions:**
1. Increase LinkedIn Ads budget by $10,000 (pull from cross-sell email budget which should be performing by then)
2. Launch BDR-assisted outbound: Hunting AEs dedicate 2 hours/week to manual LinkedIn outreach using sales enablement materials
3. Activate second partner co-webinar specifically targeting net-new audience

### Risk 3: Cross-Sell Conversion Below Expectations

**Decision gate:** If fewer than 25 cross-sell demos by Week 6.

**Corrective actions:**
1. Deploy in-app notification to CRM analytics users highlighting forecasting module
2. CSM 1:1 outreach to top 50 usage accounts with personalized demo offer
3. Limited-time bundled pricing for existing CRM analytics customers
