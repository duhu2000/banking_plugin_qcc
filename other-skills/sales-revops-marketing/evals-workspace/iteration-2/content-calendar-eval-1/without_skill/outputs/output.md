## Competitive Positioning Guidelines

**DO:**
- Define the "AI-native" category clearly and repeatedly (Weeks 3-4 are critical)
- Compare against approaches and architectures, not named competitors
- Use phrases: "AI-native vs. AI-added," "analysis-first," "built on AI, not bolted on"
- Show concrete capability gaps with data (speed, accuracy, risk detection depth)
- Let pilot customers make the comparison for you in testimonials

**DO NOT:**
- Name Ironclad directly in any content (let the buyer make the connection)
- Make feature-for-feature comparison charts with named competitors
- Disparage competitors -- elevate ClauseIQ's approach instead
- Claim "the only" or "the first" unless legally defensible

**Key differentiators to reinforce in every piece:**

1. **Analysis-first architecture:** ClauseIQ understands contracts at a semantic level, not just keyword matching
2. **Cross-clause risk detection:** Identifies conflicts and dependencies between clauses that linear review misses
3. **Mid-market fit:** Enterprise-grade AI without enterprise-grade complexity or cost
4. **Speed to value:** Implementation in days, not months
