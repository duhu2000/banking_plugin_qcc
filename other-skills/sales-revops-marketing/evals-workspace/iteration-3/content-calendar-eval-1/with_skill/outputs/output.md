## Content Calendar: ClauseIQ AI-Powered Contract Management Launch

### Campaign Parameters

- **Product:** ClauseIQ -- AI-powered contract management platform
- **Target:** Mid-market legal ops teams (200-1000 employees)
- **Campaign Goal:** Demo requests
- **Duration:** 8 weeks (Weeks 1-4: pre-launch awareness; Weeks 5-8: post-launch conversion)
- **Tentpole Event:** LegalTech Connect -- Week 3 (CEO speaking)
- **Personas:**
  - **Persona A -- VP Legal Ops:** Cares about efficiency and risk reduction
  - **Persona B -- Head of Procurement:** Cares about cost and compliance
- **Committed Channels:** LinkedIn company page, CEO personal LinkedIn, Blog, Email newsletter
- **Production Resources:** 1 full-time writer + AI tooling

### Workload Calculation

**AI-assisted range: 6-9 pieces/week** (1 full-time writer with AI = 2-3x multiplier)

Target: 7 pieces/week average across 8 weeks = ~56 total pieces.
Editorial review buffer: 20% of creation time allocated.
Production deadlines: 5 business days before publish date (minimum).

### Channel Cadence (Committed Channels Only)

| Channel                 | Cadence            | Notes                                      |
| ----------------------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| LinkedIn (company page) | 3x/week            | Sustainable default                        |
| CEO Personal LinkedIn   | 4-5x/week          | Ghostwritten; high personal reach          |
| Blog                    | 1-2x/week          | SEO compound value; pillar + cluster       |
| Email Newsletter        | 1-2x/month         | High-value sends only; no filler           |

**Channels NOT planned (per brief):** Webinar, Paid Social. These are not included in the calendar. If budget or ops capacity changes, webinars could be a strong mid-funnel add -- flagged as recommendation only.

### Pillar-Cluster Architecture

**Pillar 1 (Pre-Launch -- publish Week 2):**
"The Hidden Cost of Manual Contract Management: A Guide for Mid-Market Legal Teams"
- Target: Both personas (efficiency angle for VP Legal Ops, cost angle for Head of Procurement)
- Format: Long-form guide / downloadable PDF (gated for early access signups)
- Cluster pieces: 6 derived pieces across LinkedIn, CEO LinkedIn, Blog, and Email (Weeks 2-4)

**Pillar 2 (Post-Launch -- publish Week 6):**
"AI Contract Management ROI Calculator: What Mid-Market Teams Actually Save"
- Target: Both personas (risk reduction for VP Legal Ops, compliance + cost for Head of Procurement)
- Format: Interactive blog post with embedded calculator + downloadable one-pager
- Cluster pieces: 6 derived pieces across LinkedIn, CEO LinkedIn, Blog, and Email (Weeks 6-8)
