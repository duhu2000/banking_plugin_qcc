## Production Notes and Recommendations

### Pre-Production Windows

The single biggest risk to this calendar is launch week falling behind. Mitigate with these pre-production milestones:

| Milestone | Deadline | Details |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Report landing page live | April 13 | Must be ready before Week 2 report launch |
| Weeks 1-2 content finalized | April 2 | Full 16 pieces reviewed and scheduled |
| Conference content templates | April 16 | Pre-written posts with brackets for live details |
| Weeks 4-5 content finalized | April 23 | All launch week content pre-approved |
| Product launch blog finalized | April 25 | Longest lead time piece; needs product screenshots |
| Demo page / CTA destination live | May 2 | All Week 5+ CTAs point here |

### Content Repurposing Strategy

Each blog post should be broken into 2-3 LinkedIn posts (already reflected in calendar). Additional repurposing opportunities not in the main calendar:

- **Report stats** can generate 15-20 individual social posts; only 6 are scheduled above. Bank the rest for Weeks 9-12.
- **CEO conference talk** can be repurposed into a blog series (Q3 content pipeline).
- **Launch blog** can be condensed into a 1-page PDF sales leave-behind.

### What to Cut If Capacity Gets Tight

Priority order if you need to reduce volume (cut from bottom first):

1. CEO LinkedIn engagement posts (reactive/optional posts, easy to skip)
2. LinkedIn company share posts (blog link shares add volume but lower value)
3. Blog posts (move to biweekly if needed -- but this sacrifices SEO compounding)
4. NEVER cut: Report launch content (Week 2), launch announcement content (Week 5), email newsletters

### Email Newsletter Strategy

4 newsletters across 8 weeks (biweekly):

| Send | Week | Content | Goal |
|------|------|---------|------|
| 1 | Week 2 | Report launch | Drive downloads |

Do not add more email sends. At 4,200 subscribers, list health matters more than frequency. Every email must earn the open.

- "Mid-Market Contract Management Buyer's Guide" (bottom-funnel, high conversion)
