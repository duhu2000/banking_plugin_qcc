- **LinkedIn posts:** As the lead stat in at least 4 LinkedIn company + CEO posts

```
Content Piece (any channel)
    --> CTA: Download the whitepaper

            --> Immediate: whitepaper PDF delivery + thank you page
```

Downloads are NOT a terminal metric. Every download enters the ABM nurture, and the nurture is designed to produce sales conversations within 14 days.
