# AI PIPELINE FORECASTING MODULE -- GTM LAUNCH CAMPAIGN BRIEF

Goal: 60 qualified demos booked (20+ net-new logos, 40+ cross-sell) | Budget: $200,000 | Weeks 1-12 (launch quarter)
================================================================

## STRATEGY: DUAL-MOTION GTM -- THOUGHT LEADERSHIP-LED (NET-NEW) + PRODUCT-LED CROSS-SELL (INSTALL BASE)

The net-new motion uses thought leadership to earn attention from VP Sales and CRO personas who are not yet in-market -- original research on pipeline forecasting accuracy becomes the top-of-funnel magnet. The cross-sell motion leverages existing relationships and CRM analytics usage data to drive product-led expansion, using in-app triggers, CSM-led outreach, and install-base-only events to convert existing customers faster and cheaper than acquiring net-new logos.

---

## AUDIENCE DEFINITION

**Primary -- Net-New (Target: 20+ demos):**
Persona: VP Sales, CRO at B2B SaaS companies
Firmographic: 500-5,000 employees, established revenue teams, Salesforce CRM
Pain: Pipeline forecasting is manual, spreadsheet-dependent, or reliant on rep gut feel; board and investors demand forecast accuracy improvements
Channel fit: LinkedIn (professional attention), Google Ads (in-market intent), partner co-marketing (ecosystem credibility)

**Primary -- Cross-Sell (Target: 40+ demos):**
Persona: Existing CRM analytics plan customers (340 accounts)
Firmographic: Already purchased; have usage data and relationship history
Pain: Currently using CRM analytics but pipeline forecasting is a separate workflow or doesn't exist; adoption data shows which accounts are most active
Channel fit: Email (owned relationship), in-app messaging, CSM-led outreach, webinar (install-base-only preview)

**Estimated Addressable Audience:**
Net-new: ~12,000-18,000 VP Sales / CRO at SaaS companies matching firmographic criteria (LinkedIn targeting)
Cross-sell: 340 accounts, ~500-700 active users across those accounts

## SALES-MARKETING INTEGRATION

**Lead Routing -- Net-New Logos:**
- All net-new demo requests route to 4 hunting AEs via round-robin in Salesforce
- Lead scoring distinguishes net-new from cross-sell using "Existing Customer" field in Salesforce
- Scoring threshold for routing: any demo request = HOT (immediate routing); content download + ICP match = WARM (enter Salesloft sequence)
- Hunting AE SLA: respond to HOT demo request within 4 hours; schedule demo within 48 hours

**Lead Routing -- Cross-Sell:**
- All cross-sell demo requests route to 4 farming AEs matched to existing account owner in Salesforce
- CSM is CC'd on all cross-sell demo requests to provide usage context to farming AE
- Cross-sell scoring: existing customer + demo request = HOT; existing customer + content download = WARM (CSM nurture)
- Farming AE SLA: respond within 4 hours; demo within 48 hours; coordinate with CSM before demo

**Salesloft Sequence Configuration:**
- Net-new HOT: 6-touch sequence over 21 days (email + LinkedIn + phone)
- Net-new WARM: 5-touch sequence over 35 days (email + LinkedIn)
- Cross-sell WARM: 3-touch CSM-assisted sequence over 14 days (email + in-app)
- All sequences stop on reply (branch to human follow-up)

**Enrichment:**
- All new leads enriched within 24 hours of capture (trigger-based enrichment)
- Enrichment fields: company size, revenue, tech stack, Salesforce instance confirmed
- Enrich -> Score -> Route pipeline: no lead reaches an AE without enrichment + score

## RISK AND CONTINGENCY

**Primary Risk: Post-launch demand dropoff (announcement-only fizzle)**
This is the stated historical pattern -- past launches fizzled after two weeks. The campaign architecture addresses this by allocating 67.5% of budget ($135K) to sustained Weeks 4-12 demand gen. However, if the sustained motion underperforms, the campaign could still front-load results and trail off.

**Decision Gate:** If demo pace is below 15 total demos by end of Week 4, trigger contingency.
Metric to evaluate: weekly demo booking rate (target: 5+/week sustained after launch week)
Prescribed corrective action:
1. Reallocate $15K from underperforming LinkedIn awareness to retargeting (higher intent)
2. Activate partner acceleration: ask Salesforce and HubSpot partners for dedicated email send to their customer base
3. Deploy CSM blitz: farming AEs + CSMs run personalized outreach to top 100 install base accounts in Week 5-6
4. Refresh creative: new ad variants, new blog angles, new email subject lines by Week 5

**Secondary Risk: Cross-sell underperformance**
If install base demos are below 15 by Week 4, the CRM analytics usage data segmentation may be too broad.
Contingency: Narrow cross-sell outreach to top 50 highest-usage accounts with personalized CSM demos.

**Tertiary Risk: Partner co-marketing underdelivers**
Partners agree to co-market but deprioritize execution.
Contingency: Shift $10K from partner budget to Google Ads + retargeting; track partner-sourced demos weekly and escalate at Week 4 if below 5.
